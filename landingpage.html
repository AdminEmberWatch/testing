EMBER WATCH: Smoke & Fire Detection

Ember Watch is a safety awareness application designed to detect smoke and fire. Our innovative technology aims to provide individuals and businesses with an early warning system, ensuring prompt action and minimizing potential damages. With Ember Watch, safety is just a tap away.

HOW TO USE?

Discover the simple steps to enhance your safety with smoke and fire sensors. Learn to use them effectively by registering your account, strategically placing the sensor, and initiating emergency calls when needed. This concise guide empowers you to take swift and decisive actions for a secure environment.

Register your account

In order to utilize all of our system capabilities, you must first register to access the main application. Simply input your credentials.

Fire Sensor

Place your sensor where you think there may be a chance of a fire. Additionally, the sensor will alert the user's linked mobile devices if it detects smoke or fire.

Contacts

Once the fire sensor detects smoke or fire, you can contact the available fire stations on the contact lists for emergencies.
